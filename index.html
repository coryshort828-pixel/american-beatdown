FIGHT FAQ

Everything you need to know before stepping into the arena.

What are the age requirements?
All fighters must be at least 18 years of age on the day of the event. Attendees must be 18+ for VIP areas, but General Admission is open to all ages (parental discretion advised).
Do fighters need medical clearance?
Yes. Safety is our top priority. All fighters must pass a pre-fight physical on-site and obtain a $50 contestant license from the NC Boxing and Combat Sports Commission.
Where should I park?
Plenty of free parking is available directly at the Asheville Mall. Follow the event signage to the designated entry doors near the old Sears wing.
What are the rules of the match?
Bouts consist of three (3) 1-minute rounds. Standard amateur boxing rules apply: 16oz gloves, headgear required, standing 8-counts strictly enforced. No kicks, elbows, or grappling.
